Business Incentives and Economic Inclusion

  • InStore Program
  • Business loans
  • Economic Inclusion
  • Safecam Program
  • Entrepreneurs

The "InStore Program" is a forgiveable loan program that helps eligible retail, food, and creative for-profit and non-profit businesses purchase equipment and materials associated with establishing a new location or expanding at an existing one.

"Business loans" are available from an assortment of sources, ranging from local groups and partners, to city, state and federally backed loans.

"Economic Inclusion" involves the development and implementation of a robust effort to include Minority, Woman, and Disabled owned business enterprises in City Contracts, advancing economic opportunities for these businesses.

The "SafeCam Program" assists commercial property owners and business owners to install surveillance cameras, making their location safer for shoppers and the community.

Entrepreneurs seeking startup funds should look beyond the traditional lenders, such as the bank, family or friends.
